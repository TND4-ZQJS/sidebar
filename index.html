Offshore Product Guidelines

Last updated: May 2026 • Broker Operations

Welcome to the unified broker operational manual. This section houses the structural guidelines required to successfully execute multi-form processing across our offshore insurance lines.

EPP Payment Framework

When executing an EPP Payment structure via the 1-to-3 filler engine, ensure the credit card authorization parameters exactly align with the bank's processing cycles. Misalignments can result in immediate submission rejections by the carrier.

Important Note: Always check that the premium allocation percentages total exactly 100% across all selected funds prior to printing or locking the signature pad.

Labuan Compliance Architecture

As authorized brokers, all multi-form documents generated through this local gateway must align with standard offshore data retention policies. Because this system functions strictly client-side, manual backup configurations should follow your office protocols.
